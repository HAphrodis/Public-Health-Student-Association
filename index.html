About Us

University of Rwanda Public health Student Association

The University of Rwanda Public Health Students Association (URPHSA), a student Association under the University of Rwanda, It was founded in 2017 and started field work in 2017. Currently URPHSA have five Task forces which are Prevention and control of NCDs, Prevention of malaria, Adolescent and sexual reproductive health and rights, Environment health and Nutrition program.

  • Objectives

    1. Enhance and sustain full understanding and experience in the field of public health of student members during their time at university.
    2. To provide practical, leadership and management, communication, entrepreneurship, research and presentation, networking, problem solving, team work, and time management skills during time at the university.
    3. To enhance networking of students in school of public health with potential employers, other students organizations during their academic years.
    4. To contribute to the achievement of SDGs in Rwanda through working with other organizations in the field of public health.

  • Missions

    The URPHSA will provide practical, extra-curricular skills, experience and connections to student members in the field of public health with purpose to increase their employment chances after graduation. As a youth association it will also contribute to the achievement of the global sustainable development goals (SDGs) in Rwanda.

  • Vision

    To contribute to the production of skilful graduates in the public health field and reduction of unemployment rate in Rwanda.
    To boost the achievement of SDGs in Rwanda

Calculate your BMI

BMI is an estimate of body fat and a good gauge of your risk for diseases that can occur with more body fat. The higher your BMI, the higher your risk for certain diseases such as heart disease, high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, gallstones, breathing problems, and certain cancers.
